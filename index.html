About Us

Built on Faith,
Driven by Purpose

Rockit Trucking Inc. is a family-owned and operated trucking company based right here in Elizabethtown, Kentucky. We were founded on the belief that a business built on God's word will stand the test of time.

Every load we haul — every bag of rock, sand, or gravel — is done with excellence and honor to the Lord. We treat every client like family and every job like it matters, because it does.

What We Do

Our Services

We specialize in hauling the materials that build communities. Whether it's a small project or a large commercial contract, we deliver on time and with care.

🪨
Rock Hauling

From crushed limestone to large decorative boulders, we transport rock safely and efficiently to your jobsite anywhere in the region.

⏳
Sand Delivery

Need sand for construction, landscaping, or fill? We deliver bulk sand loads to businesses quickly and reliably — the right material, right on time.

🏗️
Gravel Transport

Pea gravel, road base, and drainage gravel — we haul it all. Trusted by contractors and businesses throughout Elizabethtown and surrounding areas.
